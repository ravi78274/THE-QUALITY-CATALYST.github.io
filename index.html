Brief Introduction

The Quality Catalyst is a leading Consulting, Training, Inspection & Auditing organization. Our expertise encompasses the development and improvement of the ecosystem of Quality, Health & Safety, Environment, Social compliances, Information Technology, and Sustainable growth of all types of industries and Business Units.

The organization itself follows ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System, which reflects our commitment to practice what we preach. We are well equipped with valuable assets like expertise gained from international exposure with globally known organizations.

Our vision is to attain and maintain a pioneer position in the consulting, training & auditing segment worldwide through ensuring value-added services, customer satisfaction and benchmarking.
